About Us

EDU-CARE has had a remarkable impact on the people and communities in which it works And porvides Parents with financial difficulties to send their children to prestigious schools .Through dedication, collaboration and a commitment to creating positive change, we continue to build on our previous achievements to create a South Africa we can all be proud of.

EDU-CARE is a non-profit organisation based in Cape Town, South Africa, offering educational scholarships to financially disadvantaged high school scholars in the Western Cape. EDU-CARE was established in April 2021 to provide opportunities for quality education to young high school scholars, enabling future generations of South Africans access to the tools needed to meet the demands in the workplaces of tomorrow.

Short History

EDU-CARE was founded by a young developer wanting to create a change in the young people of South Africa lives. It was founded to provide the underprivlaged students with a 60% aggregate with the funds to attend primary straight to University level. Edu-Care is a Non Governmental Organisation and aims to provide families with aid in school fees and/or University Fees.

EDU-CARE recieves its funds from various sources but mainly from Donors and various Sponsors which help these children achieve the future that they deserve. If you are willing to help a child secure their future you can follow the Get Involved which shows a donations link.
